# Birthday Briefing

Business professionals aged 30–70 consistently miss important birthdays of friends, family, and their partner — not out of carelessness, but because managing dates scattered across apps and devices is too much manual work.

**Audience:** Busy professionals in relationships with a wide circle of personal and professional contacts.

**Solution:** Birthday Briefing is a mobile app that automatically consolidates all birthday information into one place and sends timely reminders with enough lead time to act. No manual synchronization, no missed moments — just the right nudge at the right time. When the day arrives, jump straight into action — call or message in one tap, right from the reminder.
